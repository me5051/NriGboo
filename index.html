Our Heritage

Rooted in
Igbo Tradition

NRI GBOO was born from a deep love for the flavors, aromas, and stories of Igbo native cuisine. Each dish on our table traces its roots to ancient recipes passed down through generations — from the heart of Eastern Nigeria to your plate.

We source only the freshest ingredients — uziza leaves, ofe onugbu, crayfish, ede cocoyam, and smoked meats — prepared the way our grandmothers did: with patience, care, and pride.
